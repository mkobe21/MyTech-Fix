# Excel Formula Help

## What it can help with

- Writing formulas from scratch — VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP, IF, SUMIF, INDEX/MATCH, and more
- Fixing formula errors — #VALUE!, #REF!, #N/A, #NAME?
- Building and formatting pivot tables
- Setting up conditional formatting and data validation
- Creating and customizing charts
- Cleaning and organizing messy data

## How to use it

Go to **Productivity → Excel & Spreadsheets** and click **Open Excel Tool**. This opens
a chat tuned specifically for Excel — you get the exact formula you need, ready to copy
and paste, with a plain-English explanation of how it works.

Upload a screenshot of your spreadsheet for even better results. The AI will analyze your
actual data layout and give advice specific to what it sees.

## Example questions

- "Write a VLOOKUP to match customer names from Sheet1 to IDs in Sheet2"
- "My SUMIF formula returns 0 but there should be matching data"
- "Create a formula to calculate a running total in column C"
- "How do I make a dropdown list that only allows values from another sheet?"
