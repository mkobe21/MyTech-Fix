# Device Setup Help

## What it can help with

Any new consumer device — routers, mesh systems, smart home devices, printers, cameras,
phones, tablets, computers. The AI walks you through the complete setup process, accounts
for your existing ecosystem (Google Home, Apple HomeKit, Alexa, etc.), and makes sure
the device is connected and configured correctly before you're done.

## How it differs from the written setup guides

The written guides at [/setup](/setup) work well for straightforward setups. The AI tool
is better when:
- You have a specific device combination (e.g., adding a Nest thermostat that also needs
  to connect to Google Home and an existing Eero network)
- Something doesn't work as expected mid-setup
- You want guidance specific to your exact model rather than a general walkthrough

## How to use it

Go to **Productivity → New Device Setup** and click **Open Setup Tool**. Tell the AI what
device you just bought and it walks you through the entire process step by step, asking
about your existing setup along the way.
