# What is MyTech-Fix?

MyTech-Fix is an AI-powered tech support platform for home users and small businesses. Instead of calling a help line, waiting on hold, or searching through confusing forums, you describe your tech problem and get clear, step-by-step guidance specific to your setup — in seconds.

## The four pillars

**Fix** — AI troubleshooting for any tech problem: WiFi dropping, printer offline, smart home devices not connecting, computers running slow, VPN issues, and more. Describe the problem or upload a photo, and the AI walks you through the fix.

**Monitor** — A built-in network diagnostic tool that measures your speed, ping, and latency in real time. Results flow directly into your AI conversations so diagnosis is based on actual data, not guesswork.

**Maintain** — Automated monitoring for your saved devices. The system checks for firmware updates, security vulnerabilities, and end-of-life warnings — and notifies you when something needs attention, without you having to check manually.

**Productivity** — AI assistance for everyday software tasks: Excel formulas, Word document formatting, and step-by-step help setting up new devices. Available on the Home plan and above.

## What makes it different from just asking ChatGPT

- **Device memory** — MyTech-Fix remembers the devices in your profile. When you ask about your WiFi, it already knows you have an Eero 6 and gives specific guidance rather than generic advice.
- **Real diagnostic data** — The AI uses your actual speed, ping, and latency measurements, not just what you describe. This means faster, more accurate diagnoses.
- **Automated maintenance** — The system monitors your devices in the background and tells you when firmware updates or security patches are available.
- **Specialist productivity tools** — Excel, Word, and device setup tools tuned for those specific tasks.

## Who it's for

**Home users** who want tech problems solved quickly without needing to understand the technical details.

**Small businesses** who need reliable tech support for their team, device inventory management, and usage reporting — without the cost of a full-time IT person.
