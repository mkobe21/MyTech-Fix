# Creating Your Account

## Signing up

Go to [mytech-fix.com](https://www.mytech-fix.com) and click **Start Free** in the navigation. You'll be asked for your email address and a password. No credit card is required to start.

Once you confirm your email, you'll have a free trial account with 5 chat sessions — enough to try every core feature before deciding on a plan.

## Choosing a plan

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Single Use | $7.99 one-time | One specific problem, no ongoing subscription |
| Home | $9.99/month | Unlimited help for yourself or your household |
| Small Business | $29.99/month | Teams up to 5, shared history, device inventory |
| Business Plus | $49.99/month | Larger teams, advanced reporting, priority support |

All paid plans include unlimited troubleshooting, device memory, network diagnostics, maintenance monitoring, and (on Home and above) productivity tools.

## Upgrading from a free trial

When your 5 free sessions are used, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. Click **Upgrade** from the prompt, or go to **Account → Billing** at any time. Payments are handled securely by Stripe.
