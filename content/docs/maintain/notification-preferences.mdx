# Notification Preferences

## Managing your preferences

Go to **Account → Notification Preferences** to control which notifications you receive.

**Security alerts** — always on, cannot be turned off. These represent real risks to your
devices and data.

**Firmware update digest** — weekly or monthly email summarizing available firmware updates
across your devices. Default: weekly.

**Maintenance tips** — general maintenance reminders (battery checks, DNS settings,
password hygiene). Default: on.

**Monthly all-clear summary** — a monthly email confirming all your devices are up to date
when no issues were found that month. Default: on.

## In-app notification center

All alerts appear in the notification center — the bell icon in the navigation. Click it to
see all recent alerts grouped by severity, mark them as read, or open a chat session for
help with any specific issue.

The bell shows an unread count badge so you always know if something needs attention
without having to check your email.

## Email frequency

Security alerts always send immediately regardless of your digest preference — they're too
important to batch. All other notifications respect your chosen frequency (weekly or
monthly).

## Device-level status

Each device in your **Devices** page shows a status badge reflecting its current
maintenance state:

- 🔴 **Security Risk** — an unread CVE alert exists for this device
- 🟠 **Update Available** — a firmware update is available
- 🟡 **Attention Needed** — a lower-priority maintenance item exists
- 🟢 **Up to Date** — no pending issues
- ⚪ **Checking** — device model not yet in the firmware database
